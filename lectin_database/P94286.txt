CC   -!- FUNCTION: Produces cycloisomaltooligosaccharide from dextran containing
CC       7, 8 or 9 glucose units. The enzyme is specific for (1->6)-alpha-D-
CC       glucans (dextrans) and, without activity toward (1->4)-alpha-D-glucans,
CC       such as amylose. It also has no activity on oligosaccharides, such as
CC       amylopectin and pullulan, containing (1->6)-alpha-D-glucosidic linkages
CC       at branch points. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:7515357}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY:
CC       Reaction=cyclizes part of a (1->6)-alpha-D-glucan chain by formation of
CC         a (1->6)-alpha-D-glucosidic bond.; EC=2.4.1.248;
CC         Evidence={ECO:0000269|PubMed:7515357};

CC   -!- FUNCTION: Required during male meiosis for completion of spermatocyte
CC       cytokinesis and possibly also required in female germline cells. Also
CC       involved in ring canal formation in male and female germline cells. Not
CC       essential for cleavage furrow ingression but is required for
CC       contractile ring stability and the attachment of the furrowing membrane
CC       to the actomyosin ring in late telophase. Displays high binding
CC       affinity for beta-galactosides. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:21187330}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts (via N-terminus) with both members of the
CC       centralspindlin complex, Pav and Tum. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:21187330}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Midbody {ECO:0000269|PubMed:21187330}.
CC       Note=Detected in the midbody in late cytokinesis. Also detected in ring
CC       canals in testis and ovary and in the fusome.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:21187330}.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Detected in testis (at protein level). Also
CC       expressed in ovary. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:21187330}.
CC   -!- DISRUPTION PHENOTYPE: Some mutants display multinucleate spermatids,
CC       sterility and asymmetric contraction of the contractile ring with
CC       subsequent collapse of the cleavage furrow.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:21187330}.

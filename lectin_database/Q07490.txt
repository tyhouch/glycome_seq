CC   -!- FUNCTION: May have a pivotal role in cell differentiation of different
CC       cell types. Signaling could be triggered by the binding of a lectin-
CC       like ligand to the CD24 carbohydrates, and transduced by the release of
CC       second messengers derived from the GPI-anchor. Modulates B-cell
CC       activation responses. In association with SIGLEC10 may be involved in
CC       the selective suppression of the immune response to danger-associated
CC       molecular patterns (DAMPs) such as HMGB1, HSP70 and HSP90. Plays a role
CC       in the control of autoimmunity (By similarity).
CC       {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P24807, ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:P25063}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cell membrane; Lipid-anchor, GPI-anchor.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Expressed in the central nervous system, in
CC       postmitotic cells of spinal cord, hindbrain, midbrain and forebrain.
CC       Expressed in epithelium during the development of non-neural tissues.
CC       Expressed in tooth development, specifically in mesenchymal cells
CC       differentiating into odontoblast in dental papilla, as well as in the
CC       developing eye and hair follicle.
CC   -!- DEVELOPMENTAL STAGE: Detected in primitive ectoderm, mesoderm and
CC       ventral endoderm; down-regulated when organogenesis is completed.
CC   -!- PTM: Extensively O-glycosylated (By similarity). The carbohydrate
CC       structure may be regulated in a tissue-specific and developmental
CC       stage-specific manner. {ECO:0000250}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the CD24 family. {ECO:0000305}.

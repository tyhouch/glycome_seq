CC   -!- FUNCTION: Part of the tripartite ATP-independent periplasmic (TRAP)
CC       transport system YiaMNO involved in the uptake of 2,3-diketo-L-
CC       gulonate. This protein specifically binds 2,3-diketo-L-gulonate. Is not
CC       able to bind either L-ascorbate or dehydroascorbate.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:16385129}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: The complex comprises the extracytoplasmic solute receptor
CC       protein YiaO, and the two transmembrane proteins YiaM and YiaN.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Periplasm {ECO:0000269|PubMed:16385129}.
CC   -!- MASS SPECTROMETRY: Mass=33468.8; Mass_error=2; Method=Electrospray;
CC       Evidence={ECO:0000269|PubMed:16385129};
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the bacterial solute-binding protein 7 family.
CC       {ECO:0000305}.
CC   -!- CAUTION: Was originally proposed to be a subunit from an L-xylulose
CC       uptake system, but PubMed:16385129 shows that YiaO does not bind L- or
CC       D-xylulose. {ECO:0000305|PubMed:14668138}.
